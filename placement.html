Placement Team

Techno India Group by virtue of its 32+ glorious years of experience in academia coupled with a vibrant placement team has earned nationwide repute and recognition. Exceptional placement statistics is the major strength of this elite group. An abundance of collaborations and tie ups with industries and foreign institutes has helped Techno India in reaching the peak of excellence. Every college under Techno India Group has individual placement cells with dedicated placement officers at their disposal. There is a centralized placement team at Techno India Salt Lake where the members regularly monitor every ongoing placement related activity and coordinate with their peers in every unit of TIG to stay informed about the recruitment drive updates. The placement department of Techno India organizes grooming sessions, soft skill development classes and mock exam interfaces for students to pass with flying colors in the recruitment drives. Techno India leaves no stone unturned in guiding its students for building a glorious professional future. Engagement in promising job profiles and higher education is paid pivotal importance at Techno India. There is a dedicated grooming department that helps students in preparing for various competitive examinations such as CAT, MAT, GMAT, GRE, NET, GATE etc. Informing the students about the tips and tricks needed to succeed in such examinations is one of the major responsibilities of the grooming and placement team. Proficiency in languages such as French and Spanish assists students in availing scholarships while studying overseas. Eminent professors, industry professionals, mentors, HODs and principals are actively involved in these grooming programs as well. Techno India does not believe in recession; this group firmly believes that with proper grooming, professional guidance and hard albeit smart work, even “mountains can be moved”. Imparting quality education and creating skilled professionals and quality human resources is the motto of Techno India; if one has the needed skills and expertise in his/her field, jobs will automatically follow.

OVERVIEW

We take pleasure in introducing ourselves as one of the leading private Engineering and Management colleges accredited under MAKAUT & affiliated to AICTE. In line with the visions of the Techno India Group, we at Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology, endeavor to provide a thorough and meaningful education to our students enabling them to meet the exacting requirements of the industries. Indeed, our previous fourteen batches of students have done very well at Campus selections conducted by major Companies. Presently, the college offers B.Tech degree in six disciplines, namely Computer Science Engineering (CSE), Information Technology (IT), Electronics & Communication (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EE), Civil Engineering (CE) & Mechanical Engineering (ME). We also offer BCA, BBA among other UG Courses, and MBA (with four specializations like Marketing, Finance, HR and Systems), M.Tech (Geo-tech, Computer Science & ECE) & MCA degree among PG courses. The goal of Training & Placement Cell is to provide employment opportunities and world class training to our Students in Leading Organizations. We have introduced modern measures towards an all round development of the students as individuals with skills in communication, presentation, group discussion and lateral thinking to ensure their compatibility as international citizens. The institution already has the following facilities: The Cell is well equipped with adequate infrastructure in terms of auditorium for Pre -Placement Talk, group discussion and interview rooms, Wi-Fi campus for online exams etc. An excellent infrastructure for Training & Pre-placement classes a facility for online examination. Besides Training, our students get ample opportunities to prove themselves in the different campus recruitment drives, conducted by Regional, National & International Companies. We are also accredited by TCS, Infosys Campus Connect, Wipro Mission 10X Program, Capgemini & many more MNCs who are regularly coming o our campus for recruiting bright fresh graduates & post graduates.
